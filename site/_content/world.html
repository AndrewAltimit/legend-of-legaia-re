Game world

Every town, dungeon, and battlefield in the game - rendered here, in 3D, from your own disc image. Pick a location from the navigator and its full map assembles in the browser: the terrain, the buildings, the props, all textured from the real PSX VRAM. Each location also carries its enemy roster, bosses, and shops. Under the hood, every one ties back to a scene label on the disc via the CDNAME map. Nothing leaves your machine.

Pick a location to assemble its map.

The map is assembled the way the game assembles it: the scene's environment mesh pack, its .MAP object grid, the floor-height table, and the walk-ground heightfield, all decoded in the browser. Sky domes and horizon backdrops are hidden - under this camera they'd sit on top of the map and hide it. Drag to pan, right-drag to pivot, wheel to zoom.

Towns

Visitable settlements - NPC dialog, shops, the inn, and the save crystal. Each entry maps to a town*, izumi, vell, geremi, balden, rayman, conc, station, or deene CDNAME block.

Field areas & dungeons

Non-town field maps - mountains, caves, castle interiors, dream sequences, and the world-map overworld. Most use the field-pack + scene-bundle containers.

Related

Cards pull from data/gamedata/*.toml (curated enemy / boss / shop tables - see game-data reference) joined against the CDNAME map. Walkthrough name ↔ CDNAME label isn't always 1:1 (balden = Vidna, geremi = Jeremi), and a handful of field-pack labels still have no walkthrough mapping.
