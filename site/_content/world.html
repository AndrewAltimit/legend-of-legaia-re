Game world

Browse every town, dungeon, and battlefield in the game - each card shows its enemy roster, bosses, and shops, and deep-links into the asset viewer pre-filtered to that location. Under the hood, every location ties back to a scene label on the disc via the CDNAME map.

Towns

Visitable settlements - NPC dialog, shops, the inn, and the save crystal. Each entry maps to a town*, izumi, vell, geremi, balden, rayman, conc, station, or deene CDNAME block.

Field areas & dungeons

Non-town field maps - mountains, caves, castle interiors, dream sequences, and the world-map overworld. Most use the field-pack + scene-bundle containers.

Related

Cards pull from data/gamedata/*.toml (curated enemy / boss / shop tables - see game-data reference) joined against the CDNAME map. Walkthrough name ↔ CDNAME label isn't always 1:1 (balden = Vidna, geremi = Jeremi), and a handful of field-pack labels still have no walkthrough mapping.
