        About this view

        Each kingdom's overworld ships as one bundle of numbered slots on the disc - ocean texture, landmark mesh pack (slot 1), placement data. Assembled scene mode draws the mesh pack at the world coordinates the bundle's “MAN” placement data gives it; the show unplaced slot-1 TMDs toggle lays out everything the table doesn't place, sorted into best-guess classes.

        The mesh inspector browses the pack one mesh at a time, and the top-down placement map plots the parsed MAN actor positions (Drake adds live-RAM-resolved actor placements). The ocean toggle plays the disc's tile + CLUT wave animation; fog approximates the retail distance-cue pass from a LUT auto-extracted from SCUS_942.54. How each reconstruction works is on the world-overview viewer page.
