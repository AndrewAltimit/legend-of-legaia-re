Minigames & Sol Tower

Coin-economy minigames, prize-exchange counters, and the side-quest chains layered through Sol Tower (CDNAME town0d). Driven by data/gamedata/casino.toml + data/gamedata/sol_tower.toml, joined against the item / weapon / armor / accessory tables so every prize resolves to its effect text. The world and shops pages already surface part of this data; this page is where the full picture lives.

What's here

Sol Tower map
Floor-by-floor layout, B2 through Roof, plus the named side-quest chains - Mary's Diary, the Music Score reunion, Sol Fever Disco, Sol Broadway, the Genesis Tree, and the Warrior's Square Gaza fight.
Slot machines
Vidna casino + Sol Tower Muscle Dome prize counter. The two locations carry different prize ladders - the Sol counter exchanges Muscle Dome / Baka Fighter / slot coins for high-value items capped at the 100,000-coin Earth Egg.
Muscle Dome
Three difficulty courses (Beginner / Expert / Master) with fixed enemy lineups, escalating clear rewards, and gated equipment / item / magic rules. Master's War God Icon first-clear is conditional on Jette's defeat.
Baka Fighter
Rock-paper-scissors with life meters: 14 rounds of escalating button sequences. Round 5 is Xain, Round 14 is Songi. Full-clear pays 460 coins.

Sol Tower

Vertical structure with a B2 basement reached by service elevator. Each floor hosts a mix of vendors, the major minigames, and a handful of side-quests that don't fit anywhere else in the gamedata schema. The asset viewer can be pre-filtered to this scene.

Side-quest chains

Sol Tower is the hub for several multi-step quests that thread back through other towns. Each row is the sequence of beats; rewards reference items in the curated tables.

Slot machines & prize counters

The Sol rows below are the Muscle Dome prize-exchange counter on F4, not the slot machine itself - the slot machine only pays out coins. The same coin pool funds Muscle Dome entry fees, Baka Fighter games, and counter purchases.

Muscle Dome courses

Three fixed-progression arenas. Entry fee is a flat 100 coins. Restrictions are enforced at course entry - Beginner and Expert disable equipment and items but allow magic; Master adds a magic ban. Accessories are always permitted.

Baka Fighter

Rock-paper-scissors variant with life meters. Each round shows a button sequence the player must reproduce. Completing all 14 rounds pays out coins.

What this means for decomp

  • The casino + Sol Tower data are ground-truth labels for the field-VM bundle inside the town0d CDNAME block. Once the Sol overlay is captured, every entry on this page is a cross-validation target for the parsed NPC scripts, shop tables, and event triggers.
  • The Muscle Dome encounter records are likely a sequence of encounter records with the existing [3 reserved][count: u8][monster_ids: u8[count]] shape, except chained per-course. The muscle_dome_enemy element tags pin which monster IDs should carry the elemental override.
  • Baka Fighter round patterns are the input expectation for the per-round timer routine. The button-token set (Square / X / O) maps to the PSX controller's primary action buttons; the field-VM probably dispatches via a per-round bytecode block.
  • The Master Course "first clear after Jette" gate is a story-flag check, not a runtime check on Jette's HP - so the relevant story flag lives in the field-VM flag banks (one of the three 32-bit / 256-bit banks documented on the script-VM page).
